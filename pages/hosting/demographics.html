How do we use your email address?
1. We may contact you for future studies related to this research and any outcomes of this research or if we need to reach out to you for any reason.

How do we ensure your privacy?
1. This study received appropriate Institutional Review Board (IRB) approval for data collection.
2. Your data is associated with a random key and NOT your email address.
3. Your email address and demographics data are kept in a separate file from your data and stored in a secure, private cloud bucket.
4. Your email address will not be shared with anyone outside of this study or any third parties.
5. Once data collection is completed, and we no longer need to contact you, we will delete your email address.
6. Your email address will not be used for any other purpose.

Note: If you do not provide an email, you will not be entered into the drawing. But you can still participate in the study.
